Information about Hydroelectricity:-
Hydroelectricity is electrically generated through water
Hydropower supplies sixth of the world electricity.
It is more renewable and green way to generate electricity.
Even more than nuclear power.
In the late 18th century hydro- power was mostly used source.
How electricity is generated through water?
In hydropower dam, the potential energy in water stored in dam is converted into Kinetic energy of water.
Fast Flowing water is brought from the dam to the turbine at the bottom of the dam.
The Kinetic energy of the following water drives turbine.
The turbine in turn drives the generator to generate electricity.
Although water reservoir is used for power generation, it can be replenished during rainy season leading to uninterrupted power generation.
However, Hydroelectricity is very important today's generation and humans.
