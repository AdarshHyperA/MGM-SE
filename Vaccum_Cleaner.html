Vaccum Cleaner
Exploring the Science of Hygiene A Vacuum Cleaner is a Household appliance that Uses Suction to remove dust, dirt and debris from Surfaces.
Vacuum cleaner The basic principle behind this is to create a partial vacuum inside the machine, which causes air to flow through hose and nozzles into the cleaner Dirt and debris are Corried in this air stream and then trapped in the dirt filter or dustbin.
working:- A vacuum cleaner Usually has the following Components:-
Motor: A motor drives the fan which Create Suction.
fan: The fan blows air into the machine Through house and nozzles Hose and nozzles - The hose and nozzle direct the flow and allow the user to reach different Areas to clean, filter.
The filter - It collects the dirts and debris absorbs into the machine.
