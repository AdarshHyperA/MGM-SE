SOLAR PUMP
Essentially, solar powered water pump work by Converting the sun rays [Photons] to electricity that will operate the water pumps.It uses solar panels to collect the photons [units of light]From Sunlight, producing the Direct Current [DC] that provides the energy for the motor to pump water out from its source.
An inverter as used if the pump motor needs Alternating Current [AC] rather than DC water pump motor takes water from any available water source, including from underground or another water source, that can be used for irrigation.
