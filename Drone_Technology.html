Advantages:
1. A significant advantage offered by drone technology is safety.
2.Drones are equipped with sensors that con collect data of a much faster pace than traditional methods.

Disadvantages:
1.Drones can be noisy and can frighten animals, which can disrupt their natural behaviour.
2.The main disadvantage of using drones for search and rescue operations is the Unlimited battery life.
3.Weather conditions can affect drone fights.
