Welcome to Smart Street Light

Project Based on Daily use of Smart Street Light

Introduction

In this project the street light system, in which light get on when needed and lights which lights go off when not needed, currently in the whole work whole world enormous electric energy is Consumed by the street lamps, which automatically turns on when it becomes dark and automatically turn off when it becomes bright.
Working principle:
A smart street light automatically regulates light intensity based on sunset \sunrise time, daily schedule human presence traffic and weather situation. There by saving, considerable energy and burning the maintenance cost.
Advantageous:-
1)smart street light could becould be equipped radar Sensor which could detect if any object comes near the pole and then becomes brighter.
2)It could act as hub for smart application
3)It could also be equipped with charging station for electric.
Disadvantage:-
1)High cost of installation as compared to compared to conventional street lights.
2) If there are issues in sensors then it might get lit, or it might remain lit for a long time, thus wasting energy.
