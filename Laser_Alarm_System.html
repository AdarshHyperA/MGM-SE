Introduction

LASER ALARM SYSTEM:- The beam of laser light is shined across the path into some kind of receiver on the other side of the path.
When the laser beam is broken, the alarm is triggered.
This alarm can be located inside the house, Bank or it can even be a text message sent to your cell phone.

Theft and Robbery:
Theft and robbery incidents pose a significant risk to banks. Criminals may target cash holdings, valuable assets, or even harm bank employees and customers. Intruder Alarm Systems provide an early warning system, allowing prompt response and minimising the risk of loss.

ADVANTAGE:-

Invisible detection:
Laser beams are invisible to the naked eye, making them difficult for intruders to detect or bypass.

Wide coverage:
Laser light security alarms can cover a large area, making them suitable for protecting expansive outdoor spaces.

DISADVANTAGE:-
The laser security system works only if the laser is obstructed.
If the intruder passes without obstructing the laser, it is considered as a failure.
In order to secure a larger area, we need more lasers and corresponding sensors.
